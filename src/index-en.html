COVID-19 Generator of international travel certificate to mainland France

Filled in data is exclusively stored on your mobile phone or computer. No information is collected by the Ministry of the Interior. The generated pdf certificate has a QR Code. This visual barcode makes it possible to read the information on your certificate at the time it was entered. It is readable with all generic QR code readers.

This certificate must be presented to transportation companies, before boarding, by passengers travelling to mainland France. It must also be presented to border control authorities, for any type of border:

  • European external borders of France (air, maritime, land including railway connections).
  • European internal borders of France.
